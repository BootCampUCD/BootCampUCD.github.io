This chart collects geographical locations using the latitude and longitudes of a number of cities. The average temperature in each city was then plotted relative to the distance from the equator (e.g. Equator Latitue = 0).


The reported wind speed was plotted on a chart. As is commonly expected, wind speeds are moderate close to the equator. The further away from the equator, the speed and frequency of wind increases, which is typical for tropical regions.
