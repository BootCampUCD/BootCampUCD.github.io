This chart collects geographical locations using the latitude and longitudes of a number of cities. The average cloudiness and associated moisture in each city was then plotted relative to the distance from the equator (e.g. Equator Latitue = 0).


As expected, mostly clear skies ocurr close to the equator with light drizzle to moderate rain, typcial for tropical regions.
